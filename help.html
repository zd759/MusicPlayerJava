Using the JMC Login

Creating a new user

To create a user, type the desired username and password into the relevant fields and press 'Create User'. You can then use these details to login.

Logging in

In order to use the media player, you must be logged in. The application will only accept the admin user or newly created users as accepted logins.

Logging Out

To log out, press 'Log Out'. This will desable the media player.


Using the Media Player

Add Songs

To add songs, click 'Add Songs'. Choose your desired files (you can select more than one) and press 'Open'. The files will be sorted by name and displayed for you.

Playing a Song

To play a song, clcik on the song you want to play in the list.

Pausing a song

Press pause button while song is playing and it will pause at current point. To resume, click 'Resume'.

Stoping a song

Press the 'Stop' button while a song is playing

Playing the next song

Press Next while a song is playing or selected

Playing the previous song

Press Previous while a song is playing or selected

Deleting a song from the playlist

Click on the song in the display list and press delete.

Erasing the entire playlist

Click on 'Clear Playlist' and confirm 'Yes'.

Searching for a song.

Type the song title (it doesn't matter what case) in the search field and press the 'Search' button. If the song is on the list it will begin playing.


Saving and opening playlists (CSV files)

Saving songs to a CSV file

To save your playlist, click 'Save to CSV' and choose your location and file name.

Loading songs from a saved CSV file.

Press the 'Load Playlist' button and select your playlist. The songs in the file will be added to the playlist and be displayed. The CSV file selected must have the right information for this load to be successful.
